# MoveLens
Smart Transport &amp; Outing Platform – Egypt  A smart, integrated platform designed to simplify transportation and outing planning across Egyptian governorates.

## 📖 Project Overview

Smart Transport & Outing Platform is an integrated system designed to:

- Suggest the fastest and most affordable transportation routes
- Verify official fares through government-linked data
- Provide step-by-step trip guidance
- Recommend entertainment places and restaurants
- Offer personalized experiences
- Enable complaint tracking and reward participation

This platform uniquely combines transportation, entertainment, AI-based suggestions, and official fare verification into one scalable system.

### 🚦 Transportation & Routes
